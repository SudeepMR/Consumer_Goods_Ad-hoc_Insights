# Consumer Goods Business Insights & Sales Analytics
This is a Data Analytics project I have done using SQL and Power BI as part of Codebasics SQL Project Challenge.

This project focuses on solving real-world business requests for a consumer goods company using SQL and Power BI.

The project involved:
- SQL-based business analysis
- KPI tracking
- Sales and product performance analysis
- Market and customer analysis
- Power BI visualizations
- Business reporting and insight generation

## Business Requests Solved

### 1. Market Analysis
- Identified markets where customer "Atliq Exclusive" operates within the APAC region

### 2. Product Growth Analysis
- Calculated percentage increase in unique products between 2020 and 2021

### 3. Segment Performance Analysis
- Generated unique product counts for each segment
- Identified segments with highest product growth

### 4. Manufacturing Cost Analysis
- Identified products with highest and lowest manufacturing costs

### 5. Customer Discount Analysis
- Analyzed top customers receiving high average pre-invoice discounts in India for FY2021

### 6. Gross Sales Analysis
- Generated monthly gross sales reports for customer "Atliq Exclusive"

### 7. Quarterly Sales Analysis
- Identified quarter with highest total sold quantity in 2020

### 8. Channel Contribution Analysis
- Evaluated sales contribution by channel for FY2021

### 9. Product Performance Analysis
- Identified top-performing products by division based on total sold quantity

### 10. Top Products Analysis
- Identified the top 3 products in each division with the highest total sold quantity during fiscal year 2021
- Performed product ranking analysis using SQL ranking functions and sales aggregation techniques
- Evaluated division-wise product performance to identify high-demand products and sales leaders
